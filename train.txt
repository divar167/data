Baga vazraka Auramazdâ hya martiyam adâ<sep>A great god is Ahuramazda who created man
Auramazdâ hya asmânam adâ<sep>Ahuramazda who created heaven
Auramazdâ hya imâm bûmim adâ<sep>Ahuramazda who created this earth
Imâm bûmim Auramazdâ adâ<sep>This earth was created by Ahuramazda
Asmânam Auramazdâ adâ<sep>The heaven was created by Ahuramazda
Yam adâ hya šiyâtim adâ martiyahyâ. <sep> Who gave happiness to man.
Auramazdâ hya šiyâtim adâ martiyahyâ. <sep> Ahuramazda who gave happiness to man.
Auramazdâ hya Artaxšaçam xšâyathiyam akunauš. <sep> Ahuramazda who made Artaxerxes king.
Artaxšaçam xšâyathiyam akunauš a ivam parûnâm xšâyathiyam. <sep> Made Artaxerxes one king for many.
Artaxšaçam aivam parûnâm framâtâram akunauš. <sep> Made Artaxerxes one leader for all.
Adam Artaxšaçâ xšâyathiya. <sep> I am Artaxerxes, the king.
Xšâyathiya vazraka ahyâyâ bûmiyâ. <sep> The great king of this land.
Ahyâyâ bûmiyâ dûraiy apiy Artaxšaçâ xšâyathiya. <sep> Artaxerxes, the king of the earth far and wide.
Xšâyathiya xšâyathiyânâm vazraka. <sep> The great king of kings.
Xšâyathiya paruzanânâm dahyûnâm. <sep> The king of many nations.
Adam Artaxšaçâ xšâyathiya dahyûnâm. <sep> I am Artaxerxes, the king of lands.
Xšâyathiya vazrakâyâ paruzanânâm. <sep> The king of great and many peoples.
Xšayâršahyâ puça Dârayavahauš. <sep> The son of Xerxes, the son of Darius.
Dârayavahauš napâ Haxâmanišiya. <sep> The grandson of Darius, the Achaemenid.
Xšayâršahyâ xšâyathiyahyâ napâ Haxâmanišiya. <sep> The grandson of King Xerxes, the Achaemenid.
Puça Xšayâršahyâ napâ Haxâmanišiya. <sep> The son of Xerxes, the Achaemenid descendant.
Dârayavahauš puça Haxâmanišiya. <sep> The son of Darius, the Achaemenid.
